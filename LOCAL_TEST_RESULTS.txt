🔐 GETTING YOUR LINKEDIN COOKIE

1. Open LinkedIn in your browser
2. Login to your account
3. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
4. Go to: Application → Cookies → https://www.linkedin.com
5. Find cookie named: li_at
6. Copy its Value
7. Paste into Settings page in the platform

⚠️ SECURITY: Never share this cookie - it gives full access to your account
